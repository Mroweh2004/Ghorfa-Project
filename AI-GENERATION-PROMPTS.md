### Common Issues & Fixes

**Problem**: Character looks different
**Fix**: Add "same 3D character design as previous, consistent style"

**Problem**: Background not clean
**Fix**: Emphasize "pure white background, no textures, clean"

**Problem**: Pose unclear
**Fix**: Be more specific about arm/hand positions

**Problem**: Expression wrong
**Fix**: Add emotional keywords: "warm smile" "professional confidence"
